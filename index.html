The Girl Behind the Screen

  • About

    My name is Eden and I recently decided to take a coding bootcamp and pursue a career in the world of tech! I love coding and the sense of completeness that comes with accomplishing the intended end result. It’s the satisfaction of solving a puzzle and watching all the pieces come together! My goal is to apply my attention to detail, critical thinking skills, and work ethic to thrive as a web developer, continuously learn new coding platforms, and create a diverse space in tech.

  • Organization

    To create clean and concise code, organization skills come to the forefront. In meticulously planning projects before touching code, I am able to create a backend and design for the application without even writing code yet. This planning and organization enables a smooth process in which I can be confident in the programming process and with the end product.

  • Communication

    Empathy and understanding are so important when it comes to communication. I consistently strive to grow and hone my communication skills, actively listening and absorbing information when others speak. With fluid communication comes more efficient and clean problem solving, as well as deeper connections and understanding.

  • Flexibility

    When it comes to learning and troubleshooting, flexibility is key! I typically will work on multiple projects at once and believe that it makes me stronger as a developer. There is always so much to learn- flexibility and my ability to switch gears has made me absorb new information more quickly and pushes me to frame issues from new perspectives.



Hobbies
